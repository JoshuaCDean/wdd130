Overview

Purpose

Are you an aspiring writer? Have an epic tale to tell, but never written before, or perhaps you are looking for a place where writing support can be found easily. This website is made for people like you! Whether you want to find out why that pesky Conversation you are writing between characters doesn't feel natural, or you are wondering at how accurate the science behind your fantasy world is, you've come to the right place. This website is here to provide you easy to find support so that you can confidently find the answers to the questions burning a hole through your head and continue on that Epic tale you're writing. With an extensive library of videos with advice from actual published authors that is easy to navigate and scour, so that you can find the perfect answer to how to properly create an immersive magic system or other questions that may be burning a hole in your head.

Know your with words? Not to worry, this website is not merely for new writers excited to start on their new journey. With videos diving into your favorite novels, to uncover what makes them successful? what makes them unsuccessful? What caused a certain sequel to outshine it's predecessor. How can you build a fantasy setting that could exist in real life? Where can you place a civilization that would actually be appropriate in the real world? How much detail should you give some descriptions? So many questions can plague you, and they can be answered, by fellow experts and amazing writers. So whether you are just starting writing, or are a veteran to the art. Everyone will be able to learn something, or enjoy a new spark of info that this website can give.

Imagine you are unfortunately in a bout of writer's block. You're not sure what's wrong with a scene your writing. You're not sure what to do, so you try opening up this website. There you are met with a choice between diving into two libraries of videos, one dedicated to World building, the other for standard writing advice. You go to the one for World Building, cause you quite aren't sure how to make a certain town feel more realistic. Opening this up you are meant with a collection of videos, and quickly scanning them you find a section about different biomes and how to scientifically recreate them yourself, like what elements are required for making a swamp setting. You try some of these videos and quickly find why your snowy village wasn't feeling realistic! You then head to the section with your Standard Writing Advice, you've been having trouble recently getting your characters to sound realistic when they talk. You quicly scour the library and find a video labeled something about character dialogue. Upon watching the video you learn or perhaps you remember all the little details it takes to make a conversation flow realistically. Perhaps you overexaggerate character's emotions? Perhaps you're interupting your characters too much when they're speaking to interject unnecessary description. You watch the whole video through and go back to writing to implement the new things you've learned, and VIOLA!

This Website is made for writers, by a writer, with advice that extends over such a wide variety of topics and problems. Even if you don't find an answer to your specific problem you are sure to find useful advice here. Advice that will change your novel from good to great. So buckle up and prepare yourself for an assault of beautiful writing advice, worldbuilding help, and deep dives into your favorite stories.

Home page

The Home Page will contain a Header and Footer. In the body of my Home Page there will be an image related to writing, as well as a brief description on what you are to expect from the Website. Something like

"Welcome to Writing 101. Are you an aspiring writer or even an expert writer? This website has been cultivated for writers of all levels, so that they may find the answers to burning questions that haunt them. Browse a library of helpful videos made by experts, that are pointed to give you the best writing advice, and the best world building tips. So that you can turn your novel from Good to Great!"

After this there will be a preview of a few of the videos, some of the most important, or ones that many people should watch or would want to, like about Dialogue and one for World building as well. Another image will follow suit after this before the footer.
